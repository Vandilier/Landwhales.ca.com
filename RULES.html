RULES

Welcome to the Land Whales rules page! 
This page is designed to make understanding our rules simple and easy. Rules are expected to be followed by all users, regardless of their rank or time played on the server.As the owner of your Minecraft account, you are responsible for what happens while you are logged onto Origin Realms. 

If a rule is broken during your time online you'll be punished accordingly, just like everyone else. Our staff team works incredibly hard to keep Land Whales safe and enjoyable for everyone. If you find yourself being warned by a member of our team, you are expected to respect their decision and work with them to resolve the situation.

Absolutely not we want to make sure every player are playing equally without getting the advantage over the others because your using hacks such as X-RAY/ FLIGHT/ AIMBOT and many more so NO! using hacks will result in an immediet ban without warning/kick.

Nope not allowed this will result in a ban if continued otherwise you will be off with a warning or a kick, we want players to feel welcomed especially new ones so please do not disrespect and treat them like how you wanna be treated.

Are disrespectful/hate speeches allowed?

We want to keep hate speeches out of the server, always stay respectful to other players and staffs if continued you'll be getting a mute or ban so don't do it
