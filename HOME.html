About Us

Landwhales is a Minecraft: JAVA EDITION server that has a variety of minigames including Factions, Bedwars, and even Skywars. Our goal is to have a working server with many players that enjoys the server.

Stay tuned as the server is only getting started we are always constantly updating every 1 week with only a 3 man team we work together and communicate to make our progress faster.
